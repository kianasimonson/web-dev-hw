Genie Bars
Step 1: Mix together corn syrup and sugar in saucepan. Cook over medium heat, until sugar dissolves and mixture begins to boil. Remove from heat, and add peanut butter. After mixing, add rice krispies. Stir until blended together. Press mix into pan coated with cooking spray. Set aside.
Step 2: Melt chocolate and butterscotch chips together over low heat. Spread evenly over cereal mixture and let cool until firm.
Step 3: Cut into and enjoy!
